about this page ✨

this semantic search goes through a curated collection of tweets that i've gathered >_<

i made this bc i rly wanted a better search for nickcammarata's account, since his writing has been incredibly helpful to me and i find myself searching his account extremely often, but twitter search is sorta lackluster, so i thought a simple vector embedding search would do the job! and it turns out it works pretty well. thanks claude for helping :)

data sources include:
• all my bookmarks & likes (from twitter data request)
• most tweets from WystanTBS (+ likes), AskYatharth, RomeoStevens76 (+ likes), johnsonmxe (all downloaded from community archive)
• smuggled most tweets from sashachapin and some tweets from nickcammarata ^^ (ie, web-scraped with claude's help)
